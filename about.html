Who We Are

Treats and Touches is a creative small business that specializes in making customized chocolates, customized cupcakes, and event decor for all types of special occasions.

Our Story

We started with a passion for creativity and celebration. Our goal is to bring joy to people through beautifully designed treats and elegant event setups.

What We Do

We focus on three main services that help make your events memorable:

  • Customized Chocolates for gifts, events, and special surprises
  • Customized Cupcakes for birthdays, weddings, and celebrations
  • Event Decor for parties, weddings, and special occasions

Our Mission

To create high-quality, personalized products that bring happiness and make every celebration special.

Why Choose Us?

We pay attention to detail, creativity, and customer satisfaction. Every order is made with care to match your theme and expectations.
